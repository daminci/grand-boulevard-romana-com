Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available depending on availability — just ask!

How does self check-in work?

You'll receive a door code before your arrival via email or WhatsApp. No need to coordinate schedules — arrive whenever suits you.

Is there a washing machine?

Yes, a washing machine with dryer and a dishwasher is available in the apartment for your use.

Is parking available nearby?

There is no private parking, but paid street parking is available very close to the building. The Piața Română area is also well-connected by metro and public transport.

Is the apartment suitable for longer stays?

Absolutely. The apartment is well-equipped for extended stays with a kitchenette, washing machine with dryer, dishwasher, fast Wi‑Fi, and a comfortable workspace. Contact us for long-stay rates.

Can I get an invoice for my stay?

Yes, invoices are available for business travelers. Please mention this when making your booking request.

Are pets allowed?

Unfortunately, pets are not permitted in the apartment.

What is the cancellation policy?

Bookings cancelled 5 or more days before arrival receive a 100% refund. Bookings cancelled between 2 and 5 days before arrival receive a 50% refund. Bookings cancelled less than 48 hours before check-in are non-refundable.

Is the apartment quiet?

The apartment is located on a calm side street just off Calea Victoriei, away from main traffic. The building has thick walls typical of Bucharest's interwar architecture, and the windows provide good sound insulation. Quiet hours in the building are observed from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM.

Do you offer airport transfer?

We don't offer transfers directly, but getting from Henri Coandă Airport to the apartment is easy and affordable. The Express 100 bus runs every 15–30 min and stops at Piața Română — just a 2-minute walk from the door (around €1). Taxis and Uber/Bolt are also widely available and typically cost €15–25 depending on traffic.

Are there supermarkets nearby?

Yes — the closest Mega Image is a 2-minute walk away (Piața Amzei). A second Mega Image on Bulevardul Magheru and a Carrefour Market are 4–7 minutes away. For gourmet products and delicatessen items, Berezka Deli on Piața Amzei is an excellent choice.
