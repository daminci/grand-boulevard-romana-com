The Neighborhood

Piața Română is the heart of Bucharest’s creative and student life, perfectly positioned between the upscale Dorobanți area and the historic Old Town.

☕ Coffee & Brunch

  • Camera din Față – A quirky, vintage tea and coffee shop just steps away.
  • Trofic – Minimalist spot known for some of the best specialty coffee and brunch in the city.
  • Ever After – A beautiful "Instagrammable" garden for cocktails and comfort food.

🍴 Dining

  • La Placinte – Famous for traditional Moldovan pies and hearty local dishes.
  • Caju by Joseph Hadad – High-end casual dining with North African influences.
  • Simigeria Luca – For a quick, classic Romanian "covrig" (pretzel) on the go.

🏛️ Culture & Sights

  • Romanian Athenaeum – A 10-minute walk to the city's most iconic concert hall.
  • Calea Victoriei – Bucharest's oldest boulevard, perfect for weekend strolls.
  • Grădina Icoanei – A peaceful, hidden park just a few blocks away.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available depending on availability — just ask!

How does self check-in work?

You'll receive a door code before your arrival via email or WhatsApp. No need to coordinate schedules — arrive whenever suits you.

Is there a washing machine?

Yes, a washing machine is available in the apartment for your use.

Is parking available nearby?

There is no private parking, but paid street parking is available very close to the building. The Piața Română area is also well-connected by metro and public transport.

Is the apartment suitable for longer stays?

Absolutely. The apartment is well-equipped for extended stays with a kitchenette, washing machine, fast Wi‑Fi, and a comfortable workspace. Contact us for long-stay rates.

Can I get an invoice for my stay?

Yes, invoices are available for business travelers. Please mention this when making your booking request.

Are pets allowed?

Unfortunately, pets are not permitted in the apartment.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation is free up to 5 days before arrival. Bookings cancelled within 48 hours of check-in are non-refundable.
