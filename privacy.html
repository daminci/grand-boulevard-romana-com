Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who we are

Grand Boulevard Apartment is a privately owned short-term rental located in Bucharest, Romania. Contact: daminci@gmail.com | +40 726 420 648

2. What data we collect

When you submit a booking request, we collect your name, email address, check-in/check-out dates, and number of guests. We do not collect payment details on this website.

3. How we use your data

Your data is used solely to respond to your booking request and coordinate your stay. We do not use it for marketing without your explicit consent, and we never sell it to third parties.

4. Cookies

This site uses a single functional cookie to remember your cookie consent preference. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

5. Data retention

Booking enquiry data is retained for up to 12 months for administrative purposes, then deleted.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at daminci@gmail.com.

7. Third-party services

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for their data practices.

Booking request forms are processed by Formspree (Formspree, Inc.), which receives the personal data you submit (name, email, dates, number of guests) in order to deliver it to us. Formspree acts as a data processor on our behalf. Please refer to Formspree's Privacy Policy for their data practices.
